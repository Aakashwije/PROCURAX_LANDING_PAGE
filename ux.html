ProcuraX UI Showcase

Secure Login

Featuring two-factor verification, ProcuraX ensures top security with a sleek, user-friendly interface. User credentials are encrypted, session tokens are automatically refreshed, and suspicious login attempts trigger instant alerts ensuring that sensitive project data remains protected at every access point.

Dashboard

A dynamic dashboard offering real-time visibility across procurement and project progress all in one clean, intuitive view. It integrates analytics, key performance indicators, and customizable widgets, giving managers immediate insights into project timelines, budgets, supplier performance, and task progress from a single centralized hub.

Documents & Media

Upload, preview, and share documents instantly with version control, drag and drop functionality, and media previews for quick collaboration. The module supports real-time co editing, secure cloud storage, and automatic metadata tagging to simplify document retrieval and auditing.

Notes

Note down quick ideas, record field updates, and sync your notes instantly across devices with project tagging for context. Each note can be linked to specific projects, tasks, or team members, enabling quick reference and improving communication consistency across departments.

Task Management

Keep your team on track with visual progress indicators, drag-and-drop prioritization, and automated deadline reminders. Tasks can be assigned by role or department, tracked in real time, and displayed through interactive Gantt or Kanban views for improved project transparency and accountability.

Smart Calendar

Stay ahead with AI-synced schedules that automatically organize meetings, tasks, and deliveries by urgency. Integrated reminders, conflict detection, and cross-project visibility ensure resource availability and schedule optimization across the organization.

Communication

Empower real-time collaboration with chat, file sharing, tagging, and instant feedback tools for distributed teams. The communication hub supports threaded discussions, mention tagging, voice notes, and AI-powered sentiment tracking to enhance teamwork efficiency and engagement.

Procurement

Manage suppliers, purchase orders, and delivery tracking with full transparency and real-time notifications. The system automates bid evaluation, vendor performance tracking, and purchase approvals, ensuring cost efficiency and regulatory compliance throughout the procurement lifecycle.

Notifications

Get intelligent, prioritized alerts — focus on what matters most while keeping distractions minimal. The smart notification engine categorizes alerts by urgency and role, providing context-aware reminders for approvals, task deadlines, and procurement updates.

BuildAssist – AI Chatbot

Get instant help from BuildAssist — your AI-powered project companion that answers queries and streamlines operations. It integrates with all ProcuraX modules to provide natural language insights, automate repetitive actions, and deliver predictive suggestions for smoother workflows and faster decision-making.
